About

The Afro Experience is an immersive celebration of African music and culture, featuring some of the top performers in the industry. Our goal is to bring the sounds, rhythms, and energy of Africa to Swansea U.K, providing a night of entertainment that's not to be missed.

Event Details

Join us for a night of non-stop music, dance and culture as we bring the vibrant sounds of Africa to life. From the upbeat rythms of Afro-Beats and Amanpiano to the soulful melodies of highlife and everything in-between, We guarantee an unforgatble musical journey that will leave you wanting more.
